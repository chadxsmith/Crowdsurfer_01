In 2014, a team of designers and developers set out to build a tool that would help indie artists build their buzz and connect with real music fans.

Bookings are growth opportunities for blooming artists, and Crowdsurfer helps indie artists book shows at venues around the country. When artists, DJ’s and musicians search for the music they sound like, Crowdsurfer’s map shows them where that music is most popular. Indie artists can use this information to propose show dates and performances to the venues that play their music the most.

Crowdsurfer puts indie artists in front of real music fans looking for their new favorite act. Our tool exposes DJs, artists and musicians to new opportunities, locations and fans using data to show them where their music is generating the most spins. Each show booked is a step forward for indie artists looking to legitimize their music career. With show dates confirmed, artists can grow their fanbase one show at a time.

With trending music and a full show calendar, artists can can grow their buzz. Crowdsurfer bridges the gap between social chatter and offline buzz for indie artists. Users can share their booked shows via social networks, bring out new fans, and make real world connections with their new fans to build a buzz without borders.
